Queen Victoria on the Great Exhibition:

'We went to the engineering part, which was very interesting. What used to be done by hand and before took months to do is now done in a few seconds by the most beautiful iron machinery. We saw first the new cotton machines and the machine tools, which the engineering industry has made possible. We saw pumping engines and machines for every possible purpose, from purifying sugar, to extracting oil from linseed. We saw beautiful medals, which are now produced by machinery in their millions. This is all most wonderful.'
